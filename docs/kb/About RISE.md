# About RISE

RISE is a business-only fiber internet ISP based in the Philippines, with a mission to accelerate internet access across the country by delivering high-quality, reliable fiber connectivity exclusively to enterprises, government institutions, and educational organizations.

Its network strategy centers on maintaining an uncongested, resilient infrastructure with ultra-low latency, transparent capacity planning, and engineer-led support — prioritizing technical integrity over mass-market scale.

RISE's product portfolio spans dedicated and shared business internet plans, EVPL private network services, wholesale IP Transit, data center colocation, the GetaFIX Internet Exchange, and Teleport platform integration services — covering connectivity, private networking, and interconnection needs for business customers end-to-end.
