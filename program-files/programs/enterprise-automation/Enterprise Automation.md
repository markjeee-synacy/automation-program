# About

Enterprise Automation is a program at RISE.

## Goals

Automation’s ultimate goal is to accelerate the speed of operating our business so that we can perform sustainably and efficiently at a higher scale.

We achieve this through the following:

1. Structured online data on all critical business processes (Analog to Digital)
2. Operational real-time analytics and performance reporting (Digital to Intelligence)
3. Automate workflows in as many processes as possible (Intelligence at Higher Scale)
