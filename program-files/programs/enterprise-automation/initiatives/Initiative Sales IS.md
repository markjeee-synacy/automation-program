# Initiative Sales IS

## Purpose

Build an automated sales process management system based on the HubSpot platform.

Covers the following concerns:

1. Sales Development (Deal workflows)
2. Pre-Sales
3. Relationship Management
4. Sales Operations Dashboards

### 2026 — [AUTO-85](https://utinternational.jira.com/browse/AUTO-85)

- **How may we consolidate and standardized all sales metrics reporting in QuickSight.** ([AUTO-93](https://utinternational.jira.com/browse/AUTO-93))

  This is a continuation of the effort to deprecate Sales Dashboard in Airtable, and move the reporting in QuickSight.

- **How may we assign RM to Company objects in HubSpot and accurately report portfolio size using Service Records.** ([AUTO-94](https://utinternational.jira.com/browse/AUTO-94))

  This is a refinement effort to establish workflows and rules to properly assign RMs to Company, and standardized the basis in calculating portfolio size using Service Records data.
