# 2025 Initiative Supply Chain IS

## Goal

Improve the Supply Chain efficiency by implementing an integrated **procurement**, **inventory**, **warehousing**, and **installation** application that **streamlines processes**, **reduces errors**, **reduces lead time**, **better visibility**, and **optimizes resource utilization**.

Includes the following processes in the company:

1. Procurement
2. Bills Payment
3. Inventory
4. Warehousing
5. Installation

## Timeline

* 2025 H1, H2
* 2026 H1
