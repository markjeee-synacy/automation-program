# Initiative Fulfillment IS

## Purpose

Build an automated Delivery & Install management process based on the Odoo platform.

Covers the following concerns:

1. Procurement (Materials and Contractual Manpower)
2. Inventory and Warehouse
3. Pre-Project Workflows (Survey, Design, RoW)
4. Third Party Service Procurement
5. Installation
6. Activation

## Objectives

### 2026 — [AUTO-83](https://utinternational.jira.com/browse/AUTO-83)

- **How may we use an automated ERP system to manage our procurement and inventory processes.** ([AUTO-99](https://utinternational.jira.com/browse/AUTO-99))

  This is a partial objective, aimed to first transition towards a clean and accurate inventory data, before we transition the rest of the workflows.

- **How may we use an automated ERP system to manage our procurement and inventory processes.** ([AUTO-100](https://utinternational.jira.com/browse/AUTO-100))

  Aim is to move to a system managed procurement and inventory process, with a system controlled stock releasing procedure for job orders.

- **How may we use an automated system to manage our customer and site installation processes.** ([AUTO-101](https://utinternational.jira.com/browse/AUTO-101))

  Aim is to incorporate workflows by Design team, with BOM data recorded in the system, and entire lifecycle of a job order (e.g. installation) tracked in the system.
