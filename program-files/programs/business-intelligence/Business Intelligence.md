# About

Business Intelligence is a program at RISE.

## Goals

Automated, real-time reporting and analysis on all business metrics (financial and operational).

Key implementations include the following:

1. Dashboard for business metrics (e.g. established KPIs by management
2. Historical reports, including actual vs forecast reporting
3. Data quality assurance
