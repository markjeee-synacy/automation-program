# 2023 Initiative Finance Analytics

## Purpose

Build consolidated financial reporting for RISE group.

Covers the following concerns:

1. Consolidated Chart of Accounts, Profit & Loss, Balance Sheet
2. Service items from Podio (Circuit Records)
3. MRC Recon
