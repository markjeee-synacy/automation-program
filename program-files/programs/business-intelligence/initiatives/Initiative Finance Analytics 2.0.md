# Initiative Finance Analytics 2.0

## Purpose

Upgrade implementations of consolidated financial reporting.

Covers the following concerns:

1. Build Python equivalent of data extractions from Xero
2. Migrate Finance Reporting configurations from Airtable to NocoDB
3. Improve usability of Finance reporting workflows
4. Load consolidated reports in Quicksight

### 2026 — [AUTO-87](https://utinternational.jira.com/browse/AUTO-87)

- **How may we ensure a maintainable and reliable consolidated accounts reporting in Finance.** ([AUTO-98](https://utinternational.jira.com/browse/AUTO-98))
