## Slack

### Definitions

By default, all Slack channels are set to private, invite-only. Automation often handles confidential and sensitive data in the company, so participantions are pre-authorized and priviledged access only.

#### Close door channel

Private, invite-only, exclusive channel to first party.

#### Open door channel

Private, request-to-join, open to all collaborators on the topic of the channel.
