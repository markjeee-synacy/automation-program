# Values and Principles

Describes the core values and principles that guide our work in the Automation Program.

# Program Values

1. Grow Human Potential — Automation should free people to do higher-value work, not replace them. We invest in building skills, confidence, and capacity so the team grows alongside the technology.
2. Holistic, Inclusive, Timely, High-Leverage Impact — We look at the full picture — across teams, processes, and systems — and prioritize efforts that deliver meaningful results for where and when it matters most.
3. Accessible, Useable, and Transparent — Solutions should be easy to understand, easy to use, and open about how they work. If people can't access it or trust it, it doesn't matter how clever it is.
4. Safety First, Secure by Design — We don't bolt on safety after the fact. Every solution is built with operational safety and information security considered from the start, not as an afterthought.
5. Agile and Responsive Execution — We work in short, focused cycles so we can adapt quickly to changing priorities and feedback. Progress is steady, visible, and open to course correction at any point.
