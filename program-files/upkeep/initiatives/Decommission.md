# Decommission

Decommission initiatives to retire critical systems in automation.

## Objectives

### 2026 — [AUTO-103](https://utinternational.jira.com/browse/AUTO-103)

- **Decommission Airtable** ([AUTO-104](https://utinternational.jira.com/browse/AUTO-104))

  Remove our Airtable account. This is a critical initiative to eliminate our reliance on Airtable, which is a costly tool that we use for a few use cases that can be better served by other tools in our stack. The decommissioning of Airtable will involve migrating any remaining data and workflows to other tools, and ensuring that all users are trained on the new tools.

- **Decommission Causal** ([AUTO-105](https://utinternational.jira.com/browse/AUTO-105))

  Remove our Causal account. This is a critical initiative to eliminate our reliance on Causal, which is a costly tool that we use for financial modeling and forecasting. The decommissioning of Causal will involve migrating any remaining models and data to other tools, and ensuring that all users are trained on the new tools.
