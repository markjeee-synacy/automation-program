# Overview

Automation is a Technology-department program at RISE. Mission: build digital intelligence infrastructure (automation + business intelligence + network intelligence) so RISE can operate at scale with precision and agility, while delivering faster, more reliable connectivity to Philippine businesses.

## Five program functions:

**Strategy & Roadmap Development** — Assess the company's current state of automation maturity, define a target vision, and build a phased roadmap to progressively develop the organization's automation capability and capacity.

**Opportunity Identification & Development** — Proactively discover and evaluate opportunities across the business to improve operational efficiency, service quality, and sustainability through automation.

**Initiative Management & Cross-Functional Execution** — Translate approved opportunities into structured initiatives, directing and coordinating execution across departments to turn ideas into measurable outcomes.

**Governance, Standards & Reusability** — Establish automation standards, tooling guidelines, and reusable frameworks to ensure consistency, scalability, and long-term maintainability of solutions across the organization.

**Performance Tracking & Continuous Improvement** — Monitor the impact of automation initiatives against defined KPIs (cost savings, efficiency gains, error reduction), capture lessons learned, and feed insights back into the roadmap to drive continuous improvement.
